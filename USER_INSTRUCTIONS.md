# MPA Judge User Instructions

This guide is for day-of-event use. It covers what Directors and Judges need to do in the app, in plain language.

## For Directors

### 1. Sign in and find your event
1. Open the MPA Judge website.
2. Sign in with your Director account.
3. Go to your ensembles and confirm the correct event is selected.

### 2. Verify your ensembles
1. Confirm each ensemble name, class/grade, and stage assignment are correct.
2. If something looks wrong, contact Admin before judging starts.

### 3. Complete event forms
1. Open your event forms and complete every required field.
2. Review each form before submitting to confirm names, details, and selections are correct.
3. Submit all required forms before day of registration. Pizza orders are due the Monday before the event.

### 4. Review released results
1. Results are visible only after Admin releases the packet.
2. Open the released packet to view:
   - Judge tape audio
   - Written comments
   - Ratings and captions
   - Stage Performance Audio (once uploaded by Admin)

### 5. If something is missing
1. Refresh once.
2. Reopen the packet.
3. If still missing, report:
   - Ensemble name
   - Stage
   - Approximate time submitted/uploaded

## For Judges

### 1. Before the first group
1. Sign in with your Judge account.
2. Confirm your assigned position and form (for example Stage, Sight Reading).
3. Open the correct ensemble from your schedule.

### 2. Record adjudication audio
1. Click **Start Recording** when ready.
2. Speak continuously during the performance.
3. Click **Stop** when finished.
   - You do not need to stop the audio, but you can. The system will stitch all of the audio recorded together before release.
4. Confirm the recording appears in the audio player.

The system processes transcription automatically in the background. You do not need to manually start transcription.

### 3. Check transcript and scoring
1. Review transcript text for obvious issues.
2. Enter caption ratings and comments as required.
3. Confirm all required sections are complete before submitting.

### 4. Submit your evaluation
1. Click **Submit** once everything is complete.
2. Wait for confirmation that submission is successful.
3. Move to the next assigned ensemble.

Important: After submit, the evaluation locks. If you submitted in error, contact Admin immediately.

## Practical Tips

1. Keep one browser tab open for judging to avoid confusion.
2. Use a stable wired or strong Wi-Fi connection when possible.
3. Pause between groups to confirm the previous submission completed.
4. If audio controls look stuck, refresh once and reopen the ensemble.

## Common Problems and What To Do

### Audio does not appear right away
- Wait a moment and refresh once.
- Confirm you pressed **Stop** at the end of recording.

### Transcript seems delayed
- Continue scoring while it processes.
- Recheck before final submit.

### Cannot find a packet
- Confirm the correct event is active.
- Confirm you are in the correct role (Director vs Judge).
- Contact Admin if assignments/schedule appear incorrect.

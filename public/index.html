                      Sight-Reading Reminders

                      At A Glance

                      • Assign percussion parts before arrival.
                      • Pre-plan all wind part splits and section coverage.
                      • Practice sight-reading at your current registered MPA level.
                      Preparation Expectations

                      Instrumentation and Percussion Assignments

                      In the sight-reading document, there is a list of instruments included in each piece. Please be sure that before arriving at MPA, you have assigned percussion parts so your percussionists know exactly what they will be playing when they enter the sight-reading room.

                      Wind Part Splits

                      Please review and plan for any part splits ahead of time. For example, if there are only two clarinet parts and a student is assigned to Clarinet 3 in your band, you should already know whether that student will cover Clarinet 1 or 2, and how any additional clarinet players will be assigned. This applies to all sections as needed.

                      Sight-Reading Level Reminder

                      As a reminder, ensembles now sight-read at the same level at which they are registered to perform. Prior to 2024, ensembles sight-read one level below, but that is no longer the case. For best preparation, please practice sight-reading at your ensemble's current MPA level with your students.

                      Auxiliary Percussion by Grade
                      Grade / UIL Level Possible Number of Players
                      Grade 2 / UIL Level 2 1 player could play both Triangle and Crash Cymbals.
                      Grade 3 / UIL Level 3 2 players minimum to play Crash Cymbals, Suspended Cymbal, and Triangle.
                      Grade 4 / UIL Level 4 2 players minimum to play Crash Cymbals, Triangle, and Suspended Cymbal.
                      Grade 5 / UIL Level 5 1 player could play both Crash Cymbals and Suspended Cymbals.
                      Grade 6 / UIL Level 6 2 players could play Wood Block, Suspended Cymbal, and Crash Cymbals.

                      Traffic Flow Maps

                      Driving Directions to Ashley High School

                      From I-40 (Option 1 - Most Direct Route)

                      1. Start at the Eastern Terminus of I-40 (N College Rd, Wilmington, NC 28405).
                      2. Head south on NC-132 S / N College Rd for 9.4 miles.
                      3. Continue straight onto Carolina Beach Rd (US-421 S) for 2.6 miles.
                      4. Turn right onto Halyburton Memorial Parkway (Veterans Park entrance).
                      5. Turn right at the first fork (next to the soccer fields).
                      6. Follow this road to the back of the school.
                      7. Enter the loading dock area using the second entrance.

                      From I-40 (Option 2 - via Randall Pkwy & Independence Blvd)

                      This route provides a smoother flow of traffic compared to staying on College Road the entire way.

                      1. Start at the Eastern Terminus of I-40 (N College Rd, Wilmington, NC 28405).
                      2. Turn right onto Randall Parkway and continue for 1.7 miles.
                      3. Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto Independence Blvd and continue for 4.1 miles.
                      4. Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto US-421 S / Carolina Beach Rd.
                      5. Continue on Carolina Beach Rd for 3.0 miles.
                      6. At Monkey Junction, merge right to stay on Carolina Beach Rd (US-421 S).
                      7. Continue for 2.7 more miles.
                      8. Turn right onto Halyburton Memorial Parkway (Ashley High School entrance).
                      9. Turn right at the first fork (next to the soccer fields).
                      10. Follow this road to the back of the school.
                      11. Enter the loading dock area using the second entrance.

                      From US-17/US-76/US-421 Cape Fear Memorial Bridge

                      1. Continue south over the Cape Fear Memorial Bridge on US-17/US-76/US-421.
                      2. Turn right onto US-421 S / S 3rd St (signs for Carolina Beach/Kure Beach).
                      3. Continue on US-421 S for 0.9 miles.
                      4. Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto US-421 S / Carolina Beach Rd.
                      5. Pass by McDonald's (on the left in 1.5 miles).
                      6. Continue on Carolina Beach Rd for 5.7 miles.
                      7. At Monkey Junction, merge right to stay on Carolina Beach Rd (US-421 S).
                      8. Continue for 2.7 more miles.
                      9. Turn right onto Halyburton Memorial Parkway (Ashley High School entrance).
                      10. Turn right at the first fork (next to the soccer fields).
                      11. Follow this road to the back of the school.
                      12. Enter the loading dock area using the second entrance.

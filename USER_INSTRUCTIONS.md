# MPAapp User Instructions

This guide is for event-day use.

## Directors

### 1. Sign in and orient yourself
1. Open the MPAapp site.
2. Sign in with your director account.
3. Start from `Dashboard` and confirm the correct school, event, and active ensemble.

### 2. Registration vs Ensemble Workspace
- Use `Registration` for participating ensembles, declared grade, and registration-level event inputs.
- Use `My Ensembles` to open an `Ensemble Workspace` for the actual ensemble details:
  - repertoire
  - instrumentation
  - seating
  - percussion / equipment
  - lunch and check-in details

### 3. Complete your ensembles
1. Open `My Ensembles`.
2. Use `Open Workspace` or the `Next Step` action for the correct ensemble.
3. Follow the `Workspace Progress`, `Current Focus`, and `Ready Blockers` guidance.
4. Mark the workspace ready only when the required sections are complete.

### 4. Event and results information
- Use `Event Info` for site/event reference information.
- Use `Official Results Packet` to access released judge results, audio, and packet files.

### 5. If results are missing
1. Refresh once.
2. Reopen `Official Results Packet`.
3. If still missing, confirm results have actually been released by admin.

## Judges

### 1. Before the first group
1. Sign in with your judge account.
2. Open `Judge`.
3. Choose `Official Workspace` unless you are intentionally testing in practice mode.
4. Confirm you are on the correct ensemble and judge position.

### 2. Record the assessment
1. Select the ensemble.
2. Click `Start Recording`.
3. Speak normally during the performance.
4. Click `Stop` when finished.
5. Confirm the recording appears in the audio section.

### 3. Complete captions
1. Open the required caption scoring section.
2. Enter the required caption comments.
3. Assign the caption scores.
4. Watch the sticky footer for:
   - caption total
   - judge overall rating

Important:
- transcript/reference notes are optional helper content
- caption comments and scores are required
- AI assistance is optional and may be unavailable

### 4. Submit
1. Confirm all required captions are complete.
2. Click `Submit`.
3. Wait for the assessment to lock before moving on.

If you submitted in error, contact admin immediately.

## Admins

### 1. Operational workspaces
- `Dashboard`: high-level event status and next-step routing
- `Registrations`: registration and director-input review
- `Schedule & Flow`: check-in queue and stage flow
- `Live Submissions`: raw assessment review
- `Packets & Results`: official slot management and release
- `Readiness`: preflight, walkthrough, and cleanup

### 2. Live Submissions
Use this workspace to:
- review raw assessments
- reassign ensemble / judge position / form type
- officialize assessments
- exclude stray assessments
- delete non-official raw assessments

### 3. Packets & Results
Use this workspace to:
- inspect blocking judge positions
- manage per-position official results
- generate result files
- release and unrelease complete results packets

### 4. If something is wrong
- wrong association: use `Live Submissions`
- wrong official slot: use `Packets & Results`
- packet incomplete: check the explicit blocking positions

## Practical Tips

1. Use one browser tab per role when possible.
2. Refresh once before assuming data is missing.
3. For live operations, prefer the workflow cards and next-step actions over jumping around the UI manually.
